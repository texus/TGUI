enum tgui::ScrollablePanel::ScrollbarPolicy
strong

Defines when the scrollbar shows up.

Enumerator
Automatic 

Show the scrollbar only when needed (default)

Always 

Always show the scrollbar, even when the contents fit inside the panel.

Never 

Never show the scrollbar, even if the contents does not fit inside the panel.
